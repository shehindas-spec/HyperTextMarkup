COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING VADAKARA

MCA

MCA stands for Master of Computer Applications, a postgraduate degree focused on software development, computer science, and IT.

B.Tech

A B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) is a four-year undergraduate degree focused on engineering and technology that blends theoretical knowledge with practical skills.

B.Tech Diploma

A BTEC (Business and Technology Education Council) diploma is a vocational qualification focused on practical, career-specific skills, offered as an alternative to traditional academic education.
